🚫 Drug-Free Workplace Policy

Commitment to a safe, drug-free workplace

📖 Document Info

Status: ✅ Complete | Classification: Internal

📑 Our Commitment

Ujamaa DeFi Platform is committed to providing a safe, healthy, drug-free workplace for all team members.

❌ Prohibited Conduct

Not Permitted
  • Use of illegal drugs on company premises
  • Use of illegal drugs during work hours
  • Being under the influence at work
  • Possession or distribution at work
  • Alcohol abuse affecting work

💊 Prescription Medications

  • Lawful use is permitted
  • Must not impair work performance
  • Must not create safety risks
  • Discuss with manager if concerns
  • Medical confidentiality maintained

🧪 Testing

SituationTesting
Pre-employmentMay be required
Reasonable SuspicionRequired

RandomFor safety-sensitive roles
Return-to-WorkMay be required

🤝 Support

Assistance Available
  • Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
  • Substance abuse counseling
  • Medical leave options
  • Return-to-work programs

⚖️ Consequences

  • First violation: Counseling/treatment
  • Second violation: Final warning
  • Third violation: Termination
  • Safety violations: Immediate termination
  • Legal violations: Reported to authorities
