🔄 Succession Planning Guide

Ensuring leadership continuity

📖 Document Info

Status: ✅ Complete | Classification: Confidential

📑 Overview

Succession planning ensures Ujamaa DeFi Platform has qualified leaders ready to step into key roles when needed.

RoleSuccessor TypeTimeline
CEOInternal/External6-12 months

📋 Identification Process

  1. Identify critical positions
  2. Assess current talent

  4. Create development plans
  5. Provide growth opportunities
  6. Regular assessment and updates

Preparing Successors
