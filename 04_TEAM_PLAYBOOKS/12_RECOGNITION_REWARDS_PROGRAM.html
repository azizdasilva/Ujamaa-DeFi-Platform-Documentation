🏆 Recognition & Rewards Program

Recognizing and rewarding outstanding contributions

📖 Document Info

Status: ✅ Complete | Audience: All Team Members

📑 Overview

At Ujamaa DeFi Platform, we believe in recognizing and rewarding exceptional contributions. This program celebrates team members who go above and beyond.

🏅 Award Categories

AwardCriteriaFrequency
Spot AwardImmediate recognitionAs earned
Quarterly ExcellenceOutstanding quarterly performanceQuarterly
Innovation AwardCreative solutionsQuarterly
Team PlayerCollaboration & supportQuarterly
Annual ExcellenceYear-long outstanding performanceAnnually

🎁 Rewards

Recognition Types
  • Monetary: Bonuses, gift cards
  • Professional: Conference attendance, training budget
  • Public: Company-wide recognition
  • Time: Extra PTO, flexible schedule
  • Experiential: Team lunches, special events

📝 Nomination Process

  1. Submit nomination form
  2. Include specific examples
  3. Explain impact
  4. Review by committee
  5. Approval and announcement
  6. Reward distribution

📊 Selection Criteria

CriteriaWeight

🎉 Recognition Events

  • Monthly: Team shout-outs
  • Quarterly: Awards ceremony
  • Annually: Company-wide celebration
