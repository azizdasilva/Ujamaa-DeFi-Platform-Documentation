💬 Communication Guidelines

Best practices for team communication

📖 Document Info

Status: ✅ Complete | Audience: All Team Members

📑 Overview

Effective communication is essential for our distributed team. These guidelines help ensure clear, efficient, and respectful communication.

📱 Communication Channels

ChannelPurposeResponse Time
SlackDay-to-day communicationWithin 4 hours
EmailFormal communicationWithin 24 hours
GitHubCode discussionsWithin 48 hours
Video CallsMeetings, pair programmingScheduled
DocumentationKnowledge sharingAsynchronous

💬 Slack Best Practices

Effective Slack Usage
  • Use threads for discussions
  • @channel only for urgent matters
  • Set status when unavailable
  • Use appropriate channels
  • Keep messages concise

📧 Email Guidelines

  • Clear, descriptive subject lines
  • Keep emails concise
  • Use bullet points for lists
  • Include clear call-to-action
  • Reply within 24 hours

🎥 Meeting Guidelines

🌍 Time Zone Considerations

  • Respect different time zones
  • Use async communication when possible
  • Record important meetings
  • Document decisions in writing
  • Be mindful of response expectations
