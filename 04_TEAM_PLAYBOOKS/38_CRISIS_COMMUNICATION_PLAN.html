📢 Crisis Communication Plan

Managing communications during crises

📖 Document Info

Status: ✅ Complete | Classification: Confidential

📑 Overview

This plan outlines how Ujamaa DeFi Platform manages communications during crisis situations to protect reputation and maintain stakeholder trust.

🚨 Crisis Types

TypeExamplesSeverity
SecurityData breach, hackCritical
OperationalSystem outage, service disruptionHigh
ReputationalNegative media, social media crisisHigh
LegalRegulatory action, lawsuitCritical
PersonnelExecutive departure, misconductMedium-High

👥 Crisis Team

Core Team Members
  • CEO - Final decision maker
  • CTO - Technical lead
  • Legal - Legal counsel
  • Marketing/Comms - Communications lead
  • HR - Personnel issues

📞 Communication Channels

AudienceChannelTimeline
Internal TeamSlack, EmailImmediate
Users/CustomersEmail, Status pageWithin 2 hours
InvestorsEmail, CallWithin 4 hours
MediaPress releaseAs needed
RegulatorsOfficial channelsAs required

📝 Message Templates

  • Acknowledge the situation
  • Express concern/empathy
  • Explain what happened (as known)
  • Describe actions being taken
  • Provide timeline for updates
  • Provide contact for questions

⚠️ Do's and Don'ts

Communication Guidelines
  • ✅ Do respond quickly
  • ✅ Do be transparent (within legal limits)
  • ✅ Do show empathy
  • ❌ Don't speculate
  • ❌ Don't admit liability without legal review
  • ❌ Don't delay unnecessarily

📊 Post-Crisis

  • Conduct post-mortem
  • Document lessons learned
  • Update crisis plan
  • Restore stakeholder confidence
  • Monitor ongoing sentiment
