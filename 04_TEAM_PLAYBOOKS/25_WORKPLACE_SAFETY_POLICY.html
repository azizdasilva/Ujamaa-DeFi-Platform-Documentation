⛑️ Workplace Safety Policy

Commitment to a safe and healthy workplace

📖 Document Info

Status: ✅ Complete | Classification: Internal

📑 Our Commitment

Ujamaa DeFi Platform is committed to providing a safe and healthy workplace for all team members, visitors, and contractors.

✅ Safety Responsibilities

Everyone's Responsibility
  • Follow safety procedures
  • Report hazards immediately
  • Use equipment properly
  • Participate in training
  • Look out for each other

🏢 Office Safety

  • Keep walkways clear
  • Report maintenance issues
  • Use ergonomic equipment
  • Follow fire safety procedures
  • Know emergency exits

🏠 Remote Work Safety

AreaGuidelines
WorkspaceErgonomic setup, good lighting
EquipmentProper electrical safety
BreaksRegular movement, eye rest
Mental HealthWork-life balance, support

🚨 Emergency Procedures

  1. Fire: Evacuate immediately, use stairs
  2. Medical: Call emergency services, notify security
  3. Security: Follow lockdown procedures
  4. Natural Disaster: Follow designated procedures

📝 Incident Reporting

  • Report all incidents immediately
  • Complete incident report form
  • Investigation conducted
  • Corrective action taken
  • Documentation maintained

📞 Safety Contacts

  • Emergency: Local emergency services
  • Security: security@ujamaa.io
  • HR: hr@ujamaa.io
  • Safety Officer: safety@ujamaa.io
