💻 Equipment Policy

Company equipment provision and usage policy

📖 Document Info

Status: ✅ Complete | Audience: All Team Members

📑 Overview

Ujamaa DeFi Platform provides necessary equipment for team members to perform their work effectively. This policy outlines equipment provision and usage guidelines.

📦 Standard Equipment

RoleLaptopMonitorAccessories
DevelopmentMacBook Pro 16"27" 4KKeyboard, mouse, dock
DesignMacBook Pro 16"32" 4KTablet, keyboard, mouse
ManagementMacBook Pro 14"27" 4KKeyboard, mouse, dock
OperationsMacBook Air 13"24" HDKeyboard, mouse

🏠 Home Office Setup

Remote Work Support
  • Setup Stipend: $500 one-time
  • Monthly Stipend: $100/month
  • Chair: Ergonomic chair provided
  • Desk: Standing desk option available
  • Internet: Reimbursed up to $100/month

📋 Equipment Request

  1. Submit equipment request form
  2. Include business justification
  3. Manager approval
  4. IT processing (3-5 business days)
  5. Equipment delivery/setup
  6. Sign equipment agreement

🔒 Equipment Security

  • Use password/biometric protection
  • Enable full disk encryption
  • Install required security software
  • Report lost/stolen equipment immediately
  • Don't leave equipment unattended

📤 Return Policy

  • Return all equipment upon departure
  • Ship using provided packaging
  • IT will verify condition
  • Damage/loss may result in charges
