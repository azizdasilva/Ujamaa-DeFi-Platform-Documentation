📊 Performance Review Guide

Guide for performance review process

📖 Document Info

Status: ✅ Complete | Audience: All Team Members, Managers

📑 Overview

Performance reviews at Ujamaa DeFi Platform are designed to support growth, provide feedback, and align individual goals with company objectives.

📋 Review Cycle

Review TypeFrequencyParticipants
1:1 Check-insBi-weeklyManager + Employee
Quarterly ReviewEvery 3 monthsManager + Employee
360° FeedbackAnnuallyPeers + Manager + Self
Annual ReviewYearlyManager + Employee + HR

📊 Evaluation Criteria

Key Performance Areas
  • Technical Excellence: Quality of work, technical skills
  • Impact: Business impact, results delivered
  • Collaboration: Teamwork, communication
  • Leadership: Mentorship, initiative
  • Growth: Learning, skill development

📝 Review Process

  1. Self-Assessment: Employee completes self-review
  2. Peer Feedback: Colleagues provide input
  3. Manager Review: Manager evaluates performance
  4. Review Meeting: Discussion and feedback
  5. Goal Setting: Set next period goals
  6. Documentation: Record in HR system

📈 Rating Scale

RatingDescriptionDistribution
5 - ExceptionalFar exceeds expectations10%
4 - ExceedsConsistently exceeds20%
3 - MeetsMeets expectations60%
2 - DevelopingPartially meets8%
1 - BelowBelow expectations2%

🎯 Goal Setting

  • SMART goals (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound)
  • Align with team and company objectives
  • Include both technical and soft skills
  • Review and adjust quarterly
