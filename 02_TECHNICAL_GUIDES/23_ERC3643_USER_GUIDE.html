📘 ERC-3643 User Guide

Complete guide to using ERC-3643 tokens on Ujamaa DeFi Platform

ℹ️ About This Guide

This guide explains how to use ERC-3643 (T-REX) permissioned tokens on the Ujamaa DeFi Platform.

🎯 What is ERC-3643?

ERC-3643 (also known as T-REX - Token for Regulated Exchanges) is a permissioned token standard that adds identity verification and transfer compliance to standard ERC-20 tokens.

Why ERC-3643?

Unlike regular cryptocurrencies that anyone can hold, ERC-3643 tokens ensure:

  • ✅ Only verified investors can hold tokens
  • ✅ All transfers comply with regulations
  • ✅ Sanctions and restrictions are enforced
  • ✅ Full audit trail for regulators

🚀 Getting Started

Step 1: Create Your Account

  1. Visit Ujamaa DeFi Platform
  2. Click "Register"
  3. Fill in your details:
    • Email address
    • Password
    • Full name or company name
    • Jurisdiction (country)
    • Investor type (Retail/Institutional)

Step 2: Connect Your Wallet

  1. Install MetaMask browser extension
  2. Create or import your wallet
  3. Add Polygon Amoy Testnet network:
    • Network Name: Polygon Amoy
    • Chain ID: 80002
    • RPC URL: https://rpc-amoy.polygon.technology/
  4. Click "Connect Wallet" on Ujamaa DeFi Platform
  5. Approve the connection in MetaMask

Step 3: Register Your Identity

This step is REQUIRED before you can hold ULP tokens.

  1. Navigate to "Compliance" → "Identity Registration"
  2. Fill in the registration form:
    • Jurisdiction: Your country of residence (2-letter code, e.g., "KE" for Kenya)
    • Investor Type:
      • RETAIL for individual investors (€1K-€50K)
      • INSTITUTIONAL for organizations (€100K+)
  3. Upload required documents:
    • Retail Investors:
      • Government-issued ID (passport, national ID)
      • Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement)
    • Institutional Investors:
      • Certificate of incorporation
      • Business registration documents
      • Beneficial owner information
  4. Submit for verification

Step 4: Wait for Verification

  • A Compliance Officer will review your submission
  • Verification typically takes 24-48 hours
  • You'll receive an email notification when verified
  • Check status in "Compliance" → "My Identity"

Step 5: Start Investing

Once verified:

  1. Navigate to "Pool Marketplace"
  2. Choose a pool to invest in
  3. Enter investment amount
  4. Confirm transaction in MetaMask
  5. Receive ULP tokens in your wallet

🔐 Identity Verification Status

Your identity can have these statuses:

Status Meaning Can Hold Tokens? Can Transfer?
NONE Not registered ❌ No ❌ No
PENDING Submitted, awaiting review ❌ No ❌ No
VERIFIED Approved by compliance officer ✅ Yes ✅ Yes
REJECTED Application rejected ❌ No ❌ No
REVOKED Previously verified, now revoked ❌ No ❌ No

💸 Transferring ULP Tokens

All ULP token transfers are validated against compliance rules:

Transfer Requirements

For a transfer to succeed, ALL conditions must be met:

  1. ✅ Sender is verified - Your identity must be VERIFIED
  2. ✅ Recipient is verified - Recipient's identity must be VERIFIED
  3. ✅ Jurisdictions allowed - Both parties must be from allowed jurisdictions
  4. ✅ Not blocked - Neither party is on blocked address list
  5. ✅ Within limits - Transfer amount is within investment limits

If Transfer Fails

You'll see an error message explaining why:

Error Message Meaning Solution
"Sender identity not verified" Your identity is not verified Complete identity registration
"Recipient identity not verified" Recipient is not verified Ask recipient to verify identity
"Blocked jurisdiction: XX" One party is from blocked country Cannot transfer to/from that jurisdiction
"Address is blocked" One party is sanctioned Cannot transfer (sanctions compliance)
"Amount exceeds investment limit" Transfer too large Reduce amount or request limit increase

🆘 Troubleshooting

"I can't hold ULP tokens"

Problem: Your tokens were transferred back or you can't receive them

Solution:

  1. Check your identity status in "Compliance" → "My Identity"
  2. If status is not VERIFIED, complete the verification process
  3. If pending, wait for compliance officer review (24-48 hours)

"My transfer failed"

Problem: Transfer transaction reverted

Solution:

  1. Check the error message for specific reason
  2. Common issues:
    • Recipient not verified → Ask them to verify
    • Jurisdiction blocked → Cannot transfer to that country
    • Amount too large → Reduce transfer amount
  3. Contact support if issue persists

"I need to update my information"

Problem: Your details have changed

Solution:

  1. Go to "Compliance" → "My Identity"
  2. Click "Update Information"
  3. Submit updated documents
  4. Wait for re-verification
