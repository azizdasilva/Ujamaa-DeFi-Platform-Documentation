📚 Learning & Development Budget

Budget and guidelines for professional development

📖 Document Info

Status: ✅ Complete | Audience: All Team Members

📑 Overview

Ujamaa DeFi Platform invests in continuous learning and development. Each team member has an annual L&D budget to support their professional growth.

💰 Budget Allocation

LevelAnnual BudgetCarryover
L1-L2 (Junior/Mid)$2,000Up to $500
L3 (Senior)$3,000Up to $750
L4-L5 (Staff+)$5,000Up to $1,000
Management$5,000Up to $1,000

✅ Approved Uses

What You Can Use It For
  • Online courses and certifications
  • Conference attendance
  • Technical books and resources
  • Workshops and training programs
  • Professional memberships
  • Coaching and mentoring

📝 Approval Process

  1. Submit L&D request form
  2. Include learning objectives
  3. Explain business relevance
  4. Manager approval
  5. HR processing
  6. Complete and share learnings
