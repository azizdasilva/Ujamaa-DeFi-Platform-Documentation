💳 Expense Reimbursement Policy

Guidelines for business expense reimbursement

📖 Document Info

Status: ✅ Complete | Audience: All Team Members

📑 Overview

This policy outlines the process for submitting and reimbursing business-related expenses incurred while working for UJAMAA DeFi Platform.

✅ Reimbursable Expenses

Approved Expenses
  • Business travel (flights, hotels, ground transport)
  • Client meals (with business purpose)
  • Conference fees and related expenses
  • Office supplies and equipment
  • Software subscriptions (work-related)
  • Home office setup (up to $500)

💰 Expense Limits

CategoryLimitNotes
Meals$75/dayItemized receipt required
Hotels$250/nightStandard room
FlightsEconomyBook in advance
Ground TransportReasonableReceipt required
Client Entertainment$150/personBusiness purpose required

📝 Submission Process

  1. Collect all receipts
  2. Submit in expense system within 30 days
  3. Include business purpose
  4. Attach all receipts
  5. Manager approval
  6. Finance review
  7. Reimbursement (7-10 business days)

❌ Non-Reimbursable

Not Covered
  • Personal expenses
  • Alcohol (unless client entertainment)
  • Entertainment without business purpose
  • Traffic violations
  • Personal phone/internet
  • Commuting expenses

📞 Support

  • Finance Team: finance@ujamaa.defi
  • Expense System: Available in HR portal
  • Processing Time: 7-10 business days
