🎓 Mentorship Program Guide

Formal mentorship program for professional growth

📖 Document Info

Status: ✅ Complete | Audience: All Team Members

📑 Overview

The UJAMAA Mentorship Program pairs experienced team members with those seeking guidance and career development support.

🎯 Program Goals

What We Aim to Achieve
  • Accelerate professional growth
  • Share institutional knowledge
  • Build stronger team connections
  • Develop leadership skills
  • Support career advancement

👥 Mentorship Types

TypeDurationFocus
Traditional6 monthsCareer development
Peer3 monthsSkill sharing
Reverse3 monthsJunior mentors senior
Group6 monthsMultiple mentees

📋 Matching Process

  1. Submit application (mentor or mentee)
  2. Complete preferences survey
  3. Review potential matches
  4. Introductory meeting
  5. Set goals and expectations
  6. Begin mentorship

📝 Expectations

For Mentors

  • Meet regularly (bi-weekly minimum)
  • Provide honest feedback
  • Share experiences and insights
  • Maintain confidentiality
  • Support mentee goals

For Mentees

  • Drive the relationship
  • Prepare for meetings
  • Be open to feedback
  • Follow through on commitments
  • Respect mentor's time

📊 Success Metrics

  • Goal achievement rate
  • Participant satisfaction
  • Retention rates
  • Promotion rates
  • Program completion rate
