🔔 Whistleblower Policy

Policy for reporting concerns and violations

📖 Document Info

Status: ✅ Complete | Classification: Confidential

📑 Overview

UJAMAA DeFi Platform encourages reporting of concerns about misconduct, violations, or unethical behavior. This policy protects whistleblowers and ensures proper investigation.

📋 Reportable Concerns

What to Report
  • Financial fraud or misconduct
  • Regulatory violations
  • Harassment or discrimination
  • Safety violations
  • Data breaches
  • Conflicts of interest
  • Other unethical behavior

🔒 Protection

Anti-Retaliation
  • No retaliation for good-faith reports
  • Confidentiality maintained
  • Anonymous reporting available
  • Protection extends to witnesses
  • Violations result in discipline

📊 Investigation Process

  1. Report received and logged
  2. Initial assessment (48 hours)
  3. Investigation assigned
  4. Evidence gathered
  5. Findings documented
  6. Corrective action taken
  7. Reporter notified (as appropriate)

⚠️ Good Faith Requirement

  • Reports must be made in good faith
  • False reports may result in discipline
  • Honest mistakes are protected
  • Malicious reports are not
