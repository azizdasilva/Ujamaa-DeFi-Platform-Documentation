🚪 Exit Interview & Offboarding Guide

Process for departing team members

📖 Document Info

Status: ✅ Complete | Audience: HR, Managers, Departing Employees

📑 Overview

This guide outlines the offboarding process for team members leaving UJAMAA DeFi Platform, ensuring a smooth transition for all parties.

📋 Offboarding Checklist

TimelineActionResponsible
2 Weeks BeforeSubmit resignationEmployee
2 Weeks BeforeTransition planManager + Employee
1 Week BeforeKnowledge transferEmployee
Last DayExit interviewHR
Last DayReturn equipmentEmployee + IT
Last DayAccess revocationIT

📝 Exit Interview

Topics Covered
  • Reason for leaving
  • Work experience feedback
  • Management feedback
  • Company culture feedback
  • Suggestions for improvement
  • Interest in returning (boomerang)

💼 Knowledge Transfer

  • Document current projects
  • Transfer files and access
  • Train replacement/colleagues
  • Update documentation
  • Share contacts and relationships

🔐 Security

Important Reminders
  • Return all company property
  • Delete company data from personal devices
  • Maintain confidentiality obligations
  • Honor non-compete agreements
  • Continue IP obligations

📦 Final Compensation

  • Final paycheck (including accrued PTO)
  • Expense reimbursements
  • Equity/token vesting status
  • Benefits continuation info
  • COBRA information (if applicable)

🤝 Staying Connected

  • Alumni network invitation
  • LinkedIn group
  • Reference policy
  • Rehire eligibility
