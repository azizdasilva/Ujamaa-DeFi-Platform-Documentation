⚖️ Conflict of Interest Policy

Policy for identifying and managing conflicts of interest

📖 Document Info

Status: ✅ Complete | Classification: Internal

📑 Overview

This policy helps identify, disclose, and manage conflicts of interest to ensure decisions are made in the best interest of UJAMAA DeFi Platform.

📋 What is a Conflict?

Conflict Situations
  • Financial interest in competitors
  • Outside employment with conflicts
  • Family relationships with vendors
  • Personal relationships affecting decisions
  • Use of company resources for personal gain
  • Accepting significant gifts

📝 Disclosure Requirements

  • Annual conflict disclosure statement
  • Immediate disclosure of new conflicts
  • Disclosure before relevant decisions
  • Update when circumstances change

📊 Review Process

StepTimelineResponsible
Disclosure SubmittedImmediateEmployee
Initial Review5 business daysManager
Assessment10 business daysLegal/HR
Decision15 business daysManagement
DocumentationOngoingHR

🔧 Management Strategies

Resolution Options
  • Recusal from related decisions
  • Divestment of financial interest
  • Modification of duties
  • Termination of outside relationship
  • Monitoring and oversight

⚠️ Prohibited Activities

  • Undisclosed conflicts
  • Using position for personal gain
  • Accepting bribes or kickbacks
  • Competing with company
  • Misusing confidential information

📞 Questions

  • Legal Team: legal@ujamaa.defi
  • HR: hr@ujamaa.defi
  • Anonymous: Available upon request
