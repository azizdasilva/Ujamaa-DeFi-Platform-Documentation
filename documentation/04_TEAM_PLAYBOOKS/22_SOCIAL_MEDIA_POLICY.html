📱 Social Media Policy

Guidelines for social media use

📖 Document Info

Status: ✅ Complete | Audience: All Team Members

📑 Overview

This policy provides guidelines for appropriate social media use, both personally and professionally, to protect Ujamaa DeFi Platform and team members.

✅ Professional Use

Official Company Accounts
  • Only authorized personnel may post
  • Follow brand guidelines
  • Get approval for sensitive topics
  • Respond professionally to comments
  • Monitor and report issues

📝 Personal Use Guidelines

Do's and Don'ts
  • ✅ Do identify views as your own
  • ✅ Do respect confidentiality
  • ✅ Do be professional and respectful
  • ❌ Don't disclose confidential information
  • ❌ Don't speak for the company
  • ❌ Don't harass or discriminate

🔐 Confidentiality

  • Don't share company secrets
  • Don't post about unreleased products
  • Don't share financial information
  • Don't discuss customers/clients
  • Don't share internal communications

⚠️ Prohibited Content

Never Post
  • Harassing or discriminatory content
  • Confidential company information
  • Defamatory statements
  • Copyrighted material without permission
  • Content that harms company reputation

  • Legal: legal@ujamaa.defi
  • HR: hr@ujamaa.defi
