©️ Intellectual Property Policy

Intellectual property ownership and protection

📖 Document Info

Status: ✅ Complete | Classification: Internal

📑 Overview

This policy defines intellectual property ownership, protection, and usage guidelines for Ujamaa DeFi Platform.

📝 IP Ownership

Company Ownership
  • All work created during employment
  • Code, documentation, designs
  • Inventions related to business
  • Company confidential information
  • Customer lists and data

🔐 Confidential Information

TypeExamplesProtection Level
Trade SecretsAlgorithms, processesHighest
TechnicalCode, architectureHigh
BusinessStrategy, financialsHigh
CustomerLists, dataHigh

📋 Employee Obligations

  • Sign IP assignment agreement
  • Maintain confidentiality
  • Disclose inventions promptly
  • Assist with IP protection
  • Return materials upon departure

⚠️ Prohibited Activities

Don't Do This
  • Disclose confidential information
  • Use company IP for personal projects
  • Copy proprietary information
  • Solicit customers after departure
  • Compete during employment

📊 IP Protection

  • Patents for inventions
  • Trademarks for branding
  • Copyrights for creative works
  • Trade secrets for processes

📞 IP Questions

  • Legal Team: legal@ujamaa.io
  • IP Disclosure: ip-disclosure@ujamaa.io
