🚨 Incident Reporting Guide

How to report incidents and concerns

📖 Document Info

Status: ✅ Complete | Audience: All Team Members

📑 Overview

This guide explains how to report various types of incidents, from security breaches to workplace concerns.

🚨 What to Report

Reportable Incidents
  • Security breaches or vulnerabilities
  • Data breaches or leaks
  • Harassment or discrimination
  • Safety incidents
  • Ethics violations
  • Financial irregularities
  • System outages

📞 Reporting Channels

Incident TypeReport ToUrgency
Securitysecurity@ujamaa.ioImmediate
Harassmenthr@ujamaa.ioImmediate
Safetysafety@ujamaa.ioImmediate
Ethicslegal@ujamaa.ioWithin 24 hours
Systemit@ujamaa.ioAs soon as possible

📝 What to Include

  • Date and time of incident
  • People involved (if known)
  • Description of what happened
  • Impact assessment
  • Evidence (screenshots, logs, etc.)
  • Witnesses (if applicable)

🔍 Investigation Process

  1. Report received and logged
  2. Initial assessment (24-48 hours)
  3. Investigation assigned
  4. Evidence gathered
  5. Interviews conducted
  6. Findings documented
  7. Action taken
  8. Reporter notified (as appropriate)

🛡️ Protection

Anti-Retaliation
  • No retaliation for good-faith reports
  • Confidentiality maintained
  • Anonymous reporting available
  • Support resources provided

📞 Emergency Contacts

  • Security Emergency: security@ujamaa.io
  • HR Emergency: hr@ujamaa.io
  • Anonymous: Available on request
