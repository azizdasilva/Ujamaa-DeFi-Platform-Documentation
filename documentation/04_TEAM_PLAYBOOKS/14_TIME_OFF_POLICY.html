🏖️ Time Off Policy

Vacation, sick leave, and time off guidelines

📖 Document Info

Status: ✅ Complete | Audience: All Team Members

📑 Overview

We believe in work-life balance and encourage team members to take time off to rest and recharge. Our flexible PTO policy supports this commitment.

🏖️ Vacation Policy

Unlimited PTO
  • Minimum: 15 days per year (encouraged)
  • Recommended: 20-25 days per year
  • Maximum: No limit (with manager approval)
  • Carryover: Not applicable

📋 Time Off Types

TypeAllowanceNotes
VacationUnlimited15 days minimum
Sick LeaveAs neededDoctor's note if >3 days
Mental HealthAs neededNo questions asked
Bereavement5 daysImmediate family
Parental Leave12 weeksPaid, all parents
Jury DutyAs requiredPaid

📝 Request Process

  1. Submit request in HR system
  2. Provide at least 2 weeks notice (vacation)
  3. Ensure coverage plan
  4. Manager approval
  5. Set out-of-office message
  6. Enjoy your time off!

⚠️ Guidelines

Important Notes
  • Coordinate with team for coverage
  • Avoid critical periods (launches, etc.)
  • Maximum 3 weeks consecutive (with approval)
  • Blackout periods may apply
  • Don't work during time off

🌍 Holidays

  • Company observes major local holidays
  • Floating holidays for cultural/religious observances
  • Company shutdown (Dec 25 - Jan 1)
