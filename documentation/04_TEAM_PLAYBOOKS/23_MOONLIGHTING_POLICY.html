🌙 Moonlighting Policy

Policy for outside employment and activities

📖 Document Info

Status: ✅ Complete | Audience: All Team Members

📑 Overview

This policy governs outside employment, consulting, and business activities to prevent conflicts of interest and ensure full commitment to Ujamaa DeFi Platform.

✅ Permitted Activities

Generally Allowed
  • Passive investments
  • Teaching or speaking engagements
  • Writing or creative work
  • Non-competing consulting
  • Board membership (non-profit)

❌ Prohibited Activities

Not Allowed
  • Working for competitors
  • Working for clients/vendors without approval
  • Using company resources for outside work
  • Activities during company time
  • Activities affecting performance

📝 Disclosure Requirements

  • Disclose all outside activities
  • Submit written request for approval
  • Update when circumstances change
  • Annual disclosure statement

📊 Approval Process

  1. Submit moonlighting request form
  2. Include details of outside activity
  3. Explain time commitment
  4. Manager review
  5. HR/Legal assessment
  6. Written approval/denial

⚖️ Considerations

FactorAssessment
Conflict of InterestMust be none
Time CommitmentMust not affect work
Company ResourcesMust not be used
ConfidentialityMust be maintained

📞 Questions

  • HR: hr@ujamaa.io
  • Legal: legal@ujamaa.io
