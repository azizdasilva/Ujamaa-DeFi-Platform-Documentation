⚖️ Work-Life Balance Guide

Maintaining healthy work-life balance

📖 Document Info

Status: ✅ Complete | Audience: All Team Members

📑 Overview

At UJAMAA DeFi Platform, we believe sustainable productivity comes from maintaining a healthy work-life balance. This guide provides tips and resources.

🕐 Working Hours

Flexible Schedule
  • Core Hours: 10 AM - 4 PM (local time)
  • Flexible Hours: Work when you're most productive
  • Time Off: Unlimited PTO policy
  • Minimum Vacation: 15 days/year encouraged

🧘 Wellness Resources

ResourceDescription
Mental Health SupportProfessional counseling services

⚠️ Burnout Prevention

Signs of Burnout
  • Chronic exhaustion
  • Decreased productivity
  • Cynicism about work
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Physical symptoms (headaches, etc.)

💡 Tips for Balance

  • Set clear boundaries
  • Take regular breaks
  • Use your vacation time
  • Disconnect after hours
  • Prioritize sleep and exercise
  • Communicate with your team

🆘 Getting Help

  • Manager: First point of contact
  • HR: hr@ujamaa.defi
  • Wellness Program: wellness@ujamaa.defi
  • Anonymous: Available upon request
