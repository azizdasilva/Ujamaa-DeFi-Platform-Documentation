This policy defines procedures for selecting, managing, and monitoring third-party vendors and service providers.

📝 Vendor Onboarding

  1. Identify business need
  2. Request for Proposal (RFP)
  3. Vendor evaluation
  4. Risk assessment
  5. Contract negotiation
  6. Due diligence
  7. Approval
  8. Onboarding

📈 Ongoing Monitoring

  • Quarterly: Performance review
  • Annually: Risk reassessment
  • As needed: Security audit review
  • Continuous: SLA monitoring

🚨 Vendor Exit

  • Data return/destruction
  • Service transition plan
  • Contract termination
  • Access revocation
  • Final assessment
