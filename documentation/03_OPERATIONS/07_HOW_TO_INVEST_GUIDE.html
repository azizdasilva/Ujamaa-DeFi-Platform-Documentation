Step 1: Add Virtual Funds

1

🏦 Simulate a Bank Wire to Your Virtual Account

What it does: Adds virtual EUR to your platform account balance. This simulates receiving a bank transfer.

How to do it:

  1. Log in to the Ujamaa platform
  2. Go to Pool Marketplace
  3. Click the 💳 Manage Funds button in the header
  4. In the modal, enter an amount (e.g., €50,000)
  5. Click Add
✅ Result: Your "Virtual Bank" balance increases. You can now use these funds to mint EUROD tokens.

Step 2: Claim to Wallet

2

🔄 Convert Virtual Balance to EUROD on the Blockchain

What it does: Deducts the amount from your virtual ledger and mints real EUROD test tokens to your connected MetaMask wallet.

How to do it:

  1. In the same Manage Funds modal, ensure MetaMask is connected
  2. Click the green 🔄 Claim & Mint EUROD button
  3. MetaMask will pop up — click Confirm
  4. Wait for the blockchain confirmation (~15 seconds on Amoy)
💡 Why two steps? This mirrors reality: your bank account (Step 1) is separate from your crypto wallet (Step 2). The "Claim" is the bridge between traditional finance and DeFi.
✅ Result: EUROD tokens now appear in your MetaMask wallet. Your virtual bank balance decreases by the claimed amount.

Step 3 & 4: Approve & Invest

3

🔒 Grant Permission (Approve EUROD)

What it does: Tells the smart contract it has permission to handle your EUROD. This is a standard Web3 security step.

How to do it:

  1. Click Invest on any pool in the marketplace
  2. Enter the amount you want to invest
  3. Click 🔒 Approve & Invest
  4. First MetaMask popup: Approve EUROD spending → Confirm
4

💰 Confirm Investment (Get uLP Tokens)

What it does: Swaps your EUROD for uLP (Liquidity Pool) tokens. These tokens represent your ownership share in the pool.

What happens next:

  1. Second MetaMask popup: Deposit EUROD → Confirm
  2. Wait for blockchain confirmation
  3. You now own uLP tokens in your wallet!
✅ Result: Your EUROD is invested. You will earn yield monthly. You can redeem your uLP tokens back to EUROD at any time.

🔙 How to Redeem (Get Your Money Back)

When you're ready to exit a pool:

  1. Go to Pool Marketplace
  2. Find the pool you invested in
  3. Click Redeem
  4. Enter the number of uLP tokens to redeem
  5. Confirm the transaction in MetaMask
✅ Result: Your uLP tokens are burned and EUROD is sent back to your wallet. You can then claim it back to your virtual bank or withdraw to your personal wallet.

🔧 Troubleshooting

❌ "Insufficient virtual balance"

You're trying to claim more than your virtual bank holds. Go back to Step 1 and add more virtual funds.

❌ "Wallet Not Connected"

Click the Connect Wallet button in the top right and select MetaMask. Ensure you're on Polygon Amoy network.

❌ "Approval failed"

Check that you have enough POL for gas fees. Get test POL from the Polygon Faucet.

❌ "Transaction stuck"

Amoy testnet can sometimes be slow. Wait 1-2 minutes. If it doesn't confirm, the transaction likely failed and you can retry.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need to sign two transactions to invest?

This is standard Web3 security. The first transaction (approve) gives the pool contract permission to handle your tokens. The second (deposit) actually moves the funds. On mainnet, you can set "unlimited approval" once and only sign the deposit for future investments.

Is my money safe?

This is a testnet demo with no real money. On mainnet, all contracts are audited and your funds are secured by smart contracts, not a central authority.

Can I invest from multiple wallets?

Each wallet is linked to one verified investor identity (KYC/KYB). You cannot use multiple wallets to bypass investment limits.

How is yield calculated?

Yield accrues daily based on the pool's target APY (10-15%). It's reflected in the increasing NAV (Net Asset Value) per uLP token. When you redeem, you get more EUROD than you invested.
