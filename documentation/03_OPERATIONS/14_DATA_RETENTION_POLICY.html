📁 Data Retention Policy

Policy for data retention and disposal

📖 Document Info

Status: ✅ Complete | Classification: Internal

📑 Overview

This policy defines data retention periods and disposal procedures for the Ujamaa DeFi Platform.

📊 Retention Schedule

Data TypeRetention PeriodLegal Basis
KYC/AML Records7 yearsRegulatory requirement
Transaction Records7 yearsRegulatory requirement
Investor Records7 years after exitRegulatory requirement
Smart Contract DataPermanentBlockchain immutable
System Logs90 daysSecurity monitoring
Financial Records7 yearsTax/Audit requirement
Employee Records7 years after terminationLabor law
Communication Records3 yearsBusiness need

🗑️ Data Disposal

Disposal Methods

  • Digital Data: Secure deletion, cryptographic erasure
  • Physical Media: Destruction, degaussing
  • Paper Records: Shredding, incineration
  • Backup Tapes: Destruction, degaussing

Disposal Process

  1. Identify data for disposal
  2. Verify retention period expired
  3. Obtain approval
  4. Execute disposal
  5. Document disposal
  6. Update records

RegulationRequirement

📊 Monitoring & Audit

  • Quarterly: Retention compliance review
  • Annually: Policy review and update
  • As needed: Regulatory change assessment
