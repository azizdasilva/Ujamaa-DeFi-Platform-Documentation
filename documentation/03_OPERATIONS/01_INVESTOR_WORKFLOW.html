🔐 Phase 1: Registration & KYC Verification

1

Create Your Account

What happens: You create an account on the Ujamaa platform, selecting your investor type:

  • RETAIL_INVESTOR — Individuals (minimum €1,000)
  • INSTITUTIONAL_INVESTOR — Companies, funds (minimum €100,000)
What gets stored in the database:
  • User record: email, password hash, wallet address, role
  • InvestorProfile record: jurisdiction, KYC status (initially PENDING), wallet address
-- Database tables affected -- INSERT INTO users (email, role, wallet_address) VALUES ('investor@email.com', 'RETAIL_INVESTOR', '0x742d...'); INSERT INTO investor_profiles (user_id, jurisdiction, kyc_status) VALUES (1, 'TG', 'pending');
↓
2

Submit KYC Documents

What happens: You upload required identity documents through the onboarding flow:

  • Individual: National ID, proof of address, selfie with ID
  • Institutional: Certificate of incorporation, tax registration, UBO declaration
Database impact: Each document creates a Document record with:
  • verification_status = PENDING
  • deadline_at = submission time + 24 hours (SLA tracking)
  • file_hash = SHA256 hash for integrity verification
⏰ 24-Hour SLA: Once you submit documents, compliance officers have 24 hours to review them. If they don't, the system flags them as "OVERDUE" automatically.
↓
3

Compliance Officer Reviews

What happens: A compliance officer sees your pending documents in their dashboard and reviews them:

  • Approve: Your kyc_status changes from PENDING to APPROVED
  • Reject: Your kyc_status changes to REJECTED with a reason noted
On-chain action (when blockchain enabled):
  • Backend calls IdentityRegistry.verifyIdentity(your_wallet_address) on Polygon Amoy
  • This writes your verification status to the blockchain — anyone can verify it on Polygonscan
✅ You're now verified! Your wallet is whitelisted to hold ERC-3643 tokens. The platform enforces this — unverified wallets cannot receive uLP tokens.

💰 Phase 2: Subscription (Investing)

4

Browse Pools & Subscribe

What happens: You browse available liquidity pools and choose one to invest in:

Pool Target APY Lock-up Min. Investment
🏭 Pool Industrie 10-12% 365 days €10,000
🌱 Pool Agriculture 12-15% 180 days €10,000
📦 Pool Trade Finance 8-10% 90 days €10,000

📝 Step-by-Step Subscription Flow:

  1. Compliance Check (Backend): Before you can invest, the backend verifies:
    • ✅ Your KYC/KYB status is APPROVED
    • ✅ Your jurisdiction is not blocked (sanctions check)
    • On-chain: IdentityRegistry.isVerified(your_wallet) returns true
  2. Balance Check (Backend): Your escrow account must have sufficient funds
    -- Database check -- SELECT balance FROM bank_accounts WHERE user_id = 1; -- If balance < investment_amount → Error: "Insufficient funds"
  3. Database Update (Backend):
    • Deduct amount from your BankAccount.balance
    • Create Investment record
    • Create/update PoolPosition with your shares
    • Increase pool's total_value
  4. Smart Contract Call (On-Chain):
    • Backend calls ULPTokenizer.deposit(amount) on Polygon Amoy
    • The contract calculates how many uLP shares you receive: shares = amount / NAV
    • At first deposit (NAV = €1.00): €10,000 → 10,000 uLP shares
    • The contract mints uLP tokens to your wallet address
🧮 How uLP Shares Are Calculated:
uLP_shares = investment_amount ÷ NAV_per_share

If NAV = €1.00 and you invest €10,000 → You get 10,000 uLP shares
If NAV = €1.05 and you invest €10,000 → You get 9,524 uLP shares
// What happens on-chain (ULPTokenizer.sol) function deposit(uint256 ujeurAmount) external returns (uint256) { uint256 uptMinted = ujeurAmount / s_navPerShare; // First deposit: 1:1 ratio if (totalSupply() == 0) { uptMinted = ujeurAmount; } s_totalPoolValue += ujeurAmount; _mint(msg.sender, uptMinted); // Transfer UJEUR from investor to pool _transferUJEURFrom(msg.sender, address(this), ujeurAmount); emit Deposit(msg.sender, ujeurAmount, uptMinted, s_navPerShare); return uptMinted; }
✅ You now own uLP tokens! These represent your share in the pool. As industrials repay their loans with interest, the NAV per share increases — so your tokens become more valuable over time.

📈 Phase 3: Earning Yield

5

How Your Investment Grows

The mechanism: Your uLP tokens don't increase in quantity — instead, their value grows through NAV appreciation.

🔄 The Yield Cycle:

  1. Industrial borrows from pool: Pool deploys €500,000 to a cotton manufacturer at 12% interest
  2. Industrial repays: After 6 months, they repay €530,000 (€500K principal + €30K interest)
  3. Yield added to pool: The €30K interest goes into the pool, increasing total value
  4. NAV recalculates: New NAV = Total Pool Value ÷ Total Shares
  5. Your share value increases: Your Value = Your uLP Balance × New NAV
Timeline Pool Value Total uLP Shares NAV/Share Your €10K Investment
Day 1 (Invest) €1,000,000 1,000,000 €1.0000 €10,000
Month 6 €1,030,000 1,000,000 €1.0300 €10,300
Year 1 €1,100,000 1,000,000 €1.1000 €11,000
🔑 Key Insight: Unlike traditional dividends, you don't receive yield as separate payments. Instead, the NAV per share compounds automatically — similar to a mutual fund. Your uLP tokens become more valuable over time.

💸 Phase 4: Redemption (Withdrawing)

6

Redeem Your uLP Shares

What happens: You decide to exit your investment and redeem your uLP shares for EUROD.

📝 Step-by-Step Redemption Flow:

  1. Validation (Backend):
    • ✅ You have a position in the pool
    • ✅ You have enough uLP shares to redeem
    • ✅ Daily withdrawal limit not exceeded (€500K)
  2. Calculation:
    EUROD_received = uLP_shares × NAV_per_share Example: 10,000 uLP × €1.10 NAV = €11,000 EUROD received
  3. Database Update:
    • Reduce your PoolPosition.shares
    • Reduce pool's total_value
  4. Smart Contract Call (On-Chain):
    • Backend calls ULPTokenizer.redeem(shares)
    • The contract burns your uLP tokens
    • Transfers EUROD back to your wallet
// What happens on-chain (ULPTokenizer.sol) function redeem(uint256 uptAmount) external returns (uint256) { // Calculate EUROD to return uint256 ujeurAmount = (uptAmount * s_navPerShare) / 1e18; s_totalPoolValue -= ujeurAmount; _burn(msg.sender, uptAmount); _transferUJEUR(msg.sender, ujeurAmount); emit Redemption(msg.sender, uptAmount, ujeurAmount, s_navPerShare); return ujeurAmount; }
✅ Redemption Complete! You've received your initial investment plus yield. The transaction is recorded on-chain with a verifiable Polygonscan link.
⚠️ Important Notes:
  • Daily withdrawal limit: €500,000 per investor per day (configurable)
  • Pool liquidity: If too many investors redeem simultaneously, the pool may not have enough liquid funds
  • Lock-up periods: Some pools have minimum lock-up (e.g., 180 days for Agriculture)

💡 Key Takeaways:
  1. Compliance First: You cannot invest without KYC approval — both in the database AND on-chain via IdentityRegistry
  2. uLP = Ownership: Your uLP tokens represent a proportional share in the entire pool's value
  3. NAV Compounds: Instead of receiving separate yield payments, your tokens appreciate in value
  4. Fully Auditable: Every action is recorded in both the database (for analytics) and on-chain (for transparency)
  5. Polygonscan Verifiable: Every transaction has a real tx hash and explorer URL — anyone can verify on-chain
