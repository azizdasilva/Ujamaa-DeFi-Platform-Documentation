📄2.1 Subscriber Representations and Warranties

The Subscriber represents and warrants to the Issuer and the Platform that:

(a) Organization and Authority (Entity Subscribers Only)

The Subscriber is a [ENTITY TYPE] duly organized, validly existing, and in good standing under the laws of [JURISDICTION]. The Subscriber has full power and authority to enter into this Agreement and to consummate the transactions contemplated hereby.

(b) Authorization

The execution, delivery, and performance of this Agreement have been duly authorized by all necessary action on the part of the Subscriber. This Agreement constitutes a valid and binding obligation of the Subscriber, enforceable against the Subscriber in accordance with its terms.

(c) Investment Purpose

The Subscriber is acquiring the Tokens for the Subscriber's own account, for investment purposes only, and not with a view to, or for sale in connection with, any distribution thereof. The Subscriber does not have any agreement or understanding, directly or indirectly, with any person to distribute or effect any distribution of any of the Tokens.

  • [ ] All Equity Owners Accredited: All equity owners of the entity are accredited investors.

  • [ ] Non-US Person: I am not a US person (as defined in Regulation S) and am outside the United States when executing this Agreement.

(e) Non-US Person (if applicable)

If the Subscriber is not an accredited investor under Rule 501, the Subscriber represents that:
- The Subscriber is not a US person (as defined in Regulation S)
- The Subscriber is outside the United States when executing this Agreement and when the buy order was originated
- No directed selling efforts have been made in the United States

(f) Access to Information

The Subscriber has received and reviewed the Offering Memorandum dated [DATE] (the "Offering Memorandum"), the Risk Disclosure Statement, and other documents related to this investment. The Subscriber has had the opportunity to ask questions of and receive answers from representatives of the Issuer and the Platform.

(g) No Registration

The Subscriber understands that the Tokens have not been registered under the Securities Act or any state securities laws and are being offered and sold pursuant to an exemption from registration. The Subscriber understands that the Tokens will be subject to transfer restrictions.

(h) Ability to Bear Risk

The Subscriber has such knowledge and experience in financial and business matters that the Subscriber is capable of evaluating the merits and risks of the proposed investment. The Subscriber is able to bear the economic risk of the investment, including the loss of the entire investment.

(i) No Governmental Approval

No registration, qualification, authorization, approval, or other action by any governmental body or agency is required for the Subscriber's execution of this Agreement or consummation of the transactions contemplated hereby, except for such filings as have been or will be made.

(j) Tax Advice

The Subscriber has consulted with or has had the opportunity to consult with the Subscriber's own tax advisor regarding the tax consequences of the investment.

(k) Anti-Money Laundering

The funds being used to purchase the Tokens are not derived from illegal activities. The Subscriber is not acting on behalf of a person or entity that is the target of economic sanctions administered by OFAC, the UN, the EU, or other applicable sanctions authorities.

(l) No Conflicts

The execution and performance of this Agreement will not conflict with or result in a breach of:
- The Subscriber's organizational documents
- Any agreement to which the Subscriber is a party
- Any law, regulation, or order applicable to the Subscriber

(m) Accuracy of Information

All information provided by the Subscriber in connection with this Subscription, including the Subscriber Information in Exhibit A, is true, complete, and accurate. The Subscriber will promptly notify the Issuer of any changes to such information.

📄3.1 Subscriber Covenants

The Subscriber covenants and agrees that:

(a) Compliance with Laws

The Subscriber will comply with all applicable laws and regulations in connection with the acquisition, holding, and disposition of the Tokens.

(b) Notification of Changes

(c) Tax Compliance

The Subscriber will be responsible for all taxes arising from the ownership, holding, or disposition of the Tokens.

(d) No Directed Selling Efforts (Non-US Investors)

The Subscriber will not engage in any directed selling efforts in the United States with respect to the Tokens.

The Issuer covenants and agrees that:

(a) Use of Proceeds

(c) Compliance

The Issuer will maintain compliance with applicable securities laws and regulations.
