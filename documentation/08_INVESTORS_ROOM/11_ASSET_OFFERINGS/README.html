📄📋 Asset Offering Process

Phase 1: Preparation

  1. Prepare Offering Documents
    - Use Offering Memorandum Template
    - Customize Term Sheet
    - Include Risk Disclosure

  2. Legal Review
    - Compliance review
    - Regulatory approval (if required)
    - Finalize documents

Phase 2: Marketing

  1. Investor Outreach
    - Target accredited investors
    - Present investment opportunity
    - Answer due diligence questions

  2. Document Distribution
    - Provide offering memorandum
    - Share term sheet
    - Explain risk factors

Phase 3: Subscription

  1. Investor Commitment
    - Investor reviews documents
    - Complete Investor Questionnaire
    - Sign Subscription Agreement

  2. Fund Transfer
    - Investor transfers funds
    - Escrow arrangement
    - Compliance verification

Phase 4: Closing

  1. Final Steps
    - Execute subscription agreement
    - Issue uLP tokens
    - Record ownership

  2. Post-Closing
    - Investor reporting begins
    - Ongoing compliance
    - Distribution payments

📄⚠️ Risk Factors

Investment Risks

Credit Risk:
- Borrower default
- Delayed repayments
- Collateral shortfall

Market Risk:
- Interest rate changes
- Economic downturns
- Currency fluctuations

Liquidity Risk:
- No secondary market
- Lock-up periods
- Redemption restrictions

Operational Risk:
- Technology failures
- Process failures
- Human error

Regulatory Risk:
- Changing regulations
- Compliance costs
- Licensing requirements

📄📝 Template Usage Guidelines

For Asset Originators

  1. Use Approved Templates
    - Start with provided templates
    - Don't modify legal language
    - Customize business terms only

  2. Legal Review Required
    - All documents reviewed by legal
    - Compliance sign-off required
    - Regulatory approval (if needed)

  3. Consistent Terminology
    - Use standard definitions
    - Follow naming conventions
    - Maintain formatting

For Investors

  1. Review Carefully
    - Read entire offering memorandum
    - Understand risk factors
    - Ask questions if unclear

  2. Seek Advice
    - Consult financial advisor
    - Legal review recommended
    - Tax advice as needed

  3. Due Diligence
    - Review track record
    - Understand underlying assets
    - Verify compliance status
