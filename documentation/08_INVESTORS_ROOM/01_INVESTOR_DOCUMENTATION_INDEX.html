📄INVESTOR JOURNEY MAP

Stage 1: Awareness

Goal: Generate initial interest

Touchpoint Document Owner
Initial Contact Executive Summary CEO
Conference Presentation Pitch Deck CEO
Website White Paper, FAQ Marketing
Referral Case Studies IR

Stage 2: Evaluation

Goal: Deep-dive into opportunity

Touchpoint Document Owner
First Meeting Investment Memorandum CEO
Follow-up DDQ Responses CFO/CCO
Technical Review White Paper, Tech DD CTO
Risk Review Risk Disclosure CCO

Stage 3: Diligence

Goal: Verify all aspects

Touchpoint Document Owner
Legal Diligence PPM, LPA, Legal Opinions Legal Counsel
Financial Diligence Financial Models, Projections CFO
Tech Diligence Audit Reports, Code Review CTO
Compliance Diligence Compliance Manual, Licenses CCO

Stage 4: Negotiation

Goal: Agree on terms

Touchpoint Document Owner
Term Sheet Discussion Term Sheet CEO
Side Letter (if applicable) Side Letter CEO/Legal
LPA Negotiation LPA Legal Counsel

Stage 5: Commitment

Goal: Close investment

Touchpoint Document Owner
Subscription Subscription Agreement Investor
KYB Documentation KYB Pack Investor/CCO
Fund Wire Wire Instructions CFO
Token Minting uLP Tokens Smart Contract

Stage 6: Ongoing Relationship

Goal: Maintain transparency, drive returns

Touchpoint Document Owner
Monthly Update Monthly Report IR
Quarterly Review Quarterly Presentation CEO/CFO
Distribution Distribution Notice CFO
Annual Meeting Annual Report Board
