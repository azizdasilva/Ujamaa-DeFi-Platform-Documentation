📄📈 The Problem: African SME Financing Gap

$331 Billion — The African SME financing gap (World Bank, 2025)

  • 17 OHADA Countries with harmonized commercial law but fragmented capital access
  • 500,000+ Industrial SMEs across OHADA zone unable to access affordable financing
  • GDIZ (Benin) (Glo-Djigbé Industrial Zone, Benin) — 14,000+ jobs created, target partner for origination
  • Traditional Banks require 100%+ collateral, 18-24% interest rates, 6-12 month approval
  • Private Equity minimum tickets ($10M+) too large for SME needs ($500K-€5M)
  • Diaspora Capital ($100B+ annual remittances) lacks institutional investment channels

Result: Productive African businesses remain undercapitalized, limiting job creation and economic development.

📄✅ The Ujamaa Solution

First ERC-3643 Compliant Liquidity Pool Platform for African Industrial Financing

Core Innovation

Component Description
uLP Token Yield-bearing ERC-3643 token representing ownership in diversified financing pool
Guarantee Token Non-transferable ERC-3643 NFT representing certified merchandise/collateral
Liquidity Pools Diversified pools (Pool Industrie, Pool Agriculture, etc.) with automated yield distribution
Fireblocks Custody Institutional-grade MPC custody for all digital assets
Bank Escrow BIIC (Banque Internationale pour l'Industrie et le Commerce)/MCB Mauritius regulated escrow accounts for fiat on/off ramps

📄📋 Subscription Process

Step-by-Step

  1. Expression of Interest → Sign NDA, receive PPM
  2. KYB Due Diligence → Submit corporate documents, UBO identification
  3. Accreditation Verification → Confirm institutional status (≥€100K)
  4. Subscription Agreement → Sign LPA, subscription documents
  5. Fund Wire → EUROD to BIIC (Banque Internationale pour l'Industrie et le Commerce)/MCB escrow account
  6. uLP Token Minting → Tokens deposited to investor wallet
  7. Yield Distribution → Quarterly EUROD distributions

Required Documents

  • Certificate of Incorporation
  • Memorandum & Articles of Association
  • Register of Directors & Shareholders
  • UBO Identification (>25% ownership)
  • Source of Funds Declaration
  • FATCA/CRS Self-Certification
  • Sanctions Declaration (OFAC/UN/EU/FATF)
