📝 Subscription Agreement Template

Investment subscription agreement template

📖 Document Info

Status: ✅ Complete | Type: Legal Template

📑 Overview

This template provides the standard subscription agreement for UJAMAA investments. Must be reviewed by legal counsel before use.

📝 Agreement Structure

SUBSCRIPTION AGREEMENT This Subscription Agreement ("Agreement") is made as of [Date] BETWEEN: [INVESTOR], with address at [Address] ("Investor") AND: UJAMAA DeFi Platform Ltd ("Issuer") ───────────────────────────────── ARTICLES: 1. Subscription and Purchase 2. Representations of Investor 3. Covenants 4. Conditions to Closing 5. Transfer Restrictions 6. Fees and Expenses 7. Governing Law 8. Miscellaneous SCHEDULES: A. Investor Information B. Accreditation Evidence C. Payment Instructions

📋 Key Provisions

ArticlePurpose
1. SubscriptionInvestment amount, security type
2. RepresentationsInvestor qualifications, authority
3. CovenantsOngoing investor obligations
4. ConditionsClosing requirements
5. Transfer RestrictionsERC-3643 compliance
6. FeesFee structure, payment

📄 Required Schedules

  • Schedule A: Complete investor information
  • Schedule B: Accreditation documentation
  • Schedule C: Payment and transfer instructions
  • Schedule D: Beneficial owners (for entities)
