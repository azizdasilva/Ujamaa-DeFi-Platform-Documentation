📄📖 Overview

This folder contains legal and investment templates for private placement memoranda, subscription agreements, term sheets, and other investment documentation.

📄📄 Documents

# Document Purpose Status
01 PPM Template Private Placement Memorandum ✅ Complete
02 Subscription Agreement Template Investment agreement ✅ Complete
03 Term Sheet Template Investment terms ✅ Complete

📄📋 Template Usage

For Legal Teams

Purpose:
- Drafting offering documents
- Standardizing legal language
- Ensuring compliance
- Reducing drafting time

Guidelines:
1. Start with approved templates
2. Customize for specific offering
3. Legal review required
4. Compliance sign-off needed

For Asset Originators

Purpose:
- Preparing investment offerings
- Presenting to investors
- Documenting terms
- Streamlining process

Guidelines:
1. Use templates as starting point
2. Fill in business terms
3. Don't modify legal provisions
4. Submit for legal review

For Investors

Purpose:
- Understanding investment terms
- Reviewing legal documents
- Comparing offerings
- Due diligence

Guidelines:
1. Read templates carefully
2. Compare across offerings
3. Seek legal advice
4. Ask questions if unclear

📄📊 Template Details

1. PPM Template (Private Placement Memorandum)

Purpose: Comprehensive offering document for investors

Sections:
- Executive Summary
- Investment Opportunity
- Risk Factors
- Management Team
- Use of Proceeds
- Financial Projections
- Legal Structure
- Subscription Procedures

Usage:
- Primary offering document
- Required for all offerings
- Legal review mandatory

Template: PPM Template

2. Subscription Agreement Template

Purpose: Legal agreement for investor commitment

Sections:
- Investor Information
- Investment Amount
- Representations & Warranties
- Accreditation Certification
- Subscription Terms
- Signatures

Usage:
- Executed by investor
- Legal binding document
- Retained in records

Template: Subscription Agreement Template

3. Term Sheet Template

Purpose: Summary of investment terms

Sections:
- Security Type
- Investment Amount
- Minimum Investment
- Expected Yield
- Fees
- Lock-up Period
- Distribution Terms
- Redemption Terms

Usage:
- Marketing document
- Non-binding (except confidentiality)
- Basis for legal documents

Template: Term Sheet Template

📄📝 Best Practices

For Template Customization

DO:
- ✅ Use latest version
- ✅ Follow formatting
- ✅ Customize business terms
- ✅ Maintain legal language
- ✅ Track changes
- ✅ Get legal review

DON'T:

- ❌ Use outdated versions
