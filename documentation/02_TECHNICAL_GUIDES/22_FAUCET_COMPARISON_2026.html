📄1. thirdweb Faucet - 500+ Networks 🥇

URL: https://thirdweb.com/faucets

Overview:

  • Total Networks: 500+ testnets
  • Claim Limit: 0.1 ETH (varies by network)
  • Frequency: 24 hours
  • Mainnet ETH: ❌ No (varies by network)
  • Account: ❌ No (wallet connection only)

Key Networks:

  • Ethereum Sepolia, Holesky, Hoodi
  • Polygon Amoy, Mumbai
  • Base Sepolia
  • Arbitrum Sepolia
  • Optimism Sepolia
  • BNB Testnet
  • Avalanche Fuji
  • Solana Testnet
    • 490+ more chains

Pros:

  • ✅ Largest network coverage (500+)
  • ✅ Universal faucet (one interface)
  • ✅ No account required (usually)
  • ✅ Developer-friendly platform

Cons:

  • ⚠️ Requirements vary by network
  • ⚠️ Some networks may require mainnet balance
  • ⚠️ Less transparent about limits

Best For: Projects deploying across many chains
