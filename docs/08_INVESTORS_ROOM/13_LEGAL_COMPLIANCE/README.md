## 🛡️ Compliance Program

### AML/CFT Program

**Components:**
1. **Customer Due Diligence (CDD)**
   - Identity verification
   - Beneficial owner identification
   - Source of funds verification

2. **Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD)**
   - PEP screening
   - High-risk jurisdictions
   - Large transactions

3. **Ongoing Monitoring**
   - Transaction monitoring
   - Sanctions screening
   - Adverse media checks

4. **Reporting**
   - Suspicious activity reports
   - Large cash transaction reports
   - Regulatory filings

### Sanctions Screening

**Screening Points:**
- Onboarding (before acceptance)
- Before each transaction
- Quarterly rescreening
- Upon sanctions list updates

**Screening Lists:**
- OFAC SDN List
- UN Consolidated List
- EU Sanctions Map
- Local sanctions lists

## ⚠️ Important Notices

### Investment Warnings

**⚠️ NO GUARANTEES:**
- Investment values can go down
- Past performance ≠ future results
- No capital protection
- Yield projections are targets, not guarantees

**⚠️ RESTRICTIONS:**
- Not available to retail investors
- Not available in all jurisdictions
- Lock-up periods apply
- Limited transferability

**⚠️ RESPONSIBILITIES:**
- Investors must maintain accreditation
- Changes in circumstances must be reported
- Tax obligations are investor's responsibility
- Compliance with local laws required
