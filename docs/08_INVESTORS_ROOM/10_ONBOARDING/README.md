## 📋 Requirements

### Accreditation Requirements

| Investor Type | Requirements |
|---------------|--------------|
| **Individual** | $1M+ net worth OR $200K+ annual income |
| **Institutional** | $5M+ assets under management |
| **Family Office** | $5M+ investable assets |

### Jurisdiction Eligibility

| Status | Jurisdictions |
|--------|---------------|
| ✅ **Approved** | Mauritius, UAE, Singapore, Switzerland |
| ⚠️ **Restricted** | USA, Canada, China (requires approval) |
| ❌ **Prohibited** | OFAC sanctioned countries |

### KYC/AML Documentation

**Required for all investors:**
- Government-issued ID (passport, national ID)
- Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement)
- Source of funds declaration
- Sanctions screening

**Additional for institutions:**
- Certificate of incorporation
- Memorandum & Articles of Association
- Board resolution for investment
- Beneficial owner disclosure
