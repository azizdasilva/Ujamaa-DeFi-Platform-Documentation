# Important Notice

**Securities laws vary by jurisdiction.** UJAMAA DEFI PLATFORM restricts access to offerings based on investor location and applicable regulations. **It is your responsibility to ensure you are eligible to invest based on your jurisdiction of residence.**

**Investing from a restricted jurisdiction may result in:**
- Rejection of subscription
- Forced redemption at NAV (potentially below purchase price)
- Account suspension
- Regulatory reporting

# 1. Jurisdiction Categories

## 1.1 Classification System

UJAMAA DEFI PLATFORM classifies jurisdictions into four categories:

| Category | Description | Investment Access |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Category A: Full Access** | Home markets with full regulatory compliance | All offerings (retail + accredited) |
| **Category B: Accredited Only** | Markets with limited regulatory framework | Accredited offerings only |
| **Category C: Restricted** | High-risk or sanctioned jurisdictions | No access |
| **Category D: Case-by-Case** | Jurisdictions requiring enhanced review | Subject to compliance approval |

## 1.2 Classification Criteria

Jurisdictions are classified based on:

| Factor | Weight | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| **Regulatory Framework** | 30% | Existence of securities regulations, STO guidelines |
| **FATF Compliance** | 25% | AML/CFT regime per FATF standards |
| **Sanctions Status** | 20% | OFAC, UN, EU sanctions |
| **Investor Protection** | 15% | Legal recourse, enforcement mechanisms |
| **Tax Information Exchange** | 10% | CRS/FATCA participation |

# 4. Offering-Specific Restrictions

## 4.1 US Offerings (Reg D 506(b))

| Restriction | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **General Solicitation** | Prohibited |
| **Investor Type** | Accredited + up to 200 non-accredited |
| **US Persons** | Permitted if accredited |
| **Non-US Persons** | Permitted if accredited or sophisticated |
| **Resale Restrictions** | Securities are restricted; no resale for 12 months |

| Restriction | Details |

# 5. Change of Jurisdiction

## 5.1 Notification Requirements

**Investors must notify UJAMAA DEFI PLATFORM within 30 days of:**

| Change | Action Required |
|--------|-----------------|
| **Change of Residence** | Update profile; new proof of address |
| **Change of Tax Residency** | Update tax information; new self-certification |
| **Change of Nationality** | Update profile; new ID document |
| **Dual Residence** | Disclose all jurisdictions of residence |

## 5.2 Impact on Existing Holdings

| Scenario | Impact |
|----------|--------|
| **Move from Category A to A** | No impact; continue holding |
| **Move from Category A to B** | Can hold existing; no new retail investments |
| **Move from Category A/B to C** | Must divest within 90 days |
| **Move to US (non-accredited)** | Must divest US-prohibited offerings |

## 5.3 Tax Withholding Changes

| Change | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **Change in Tax Residency** | May affect withholding tax rates |
| **Treaty Benefits** | Must submit new tax forms for treaty rates |
| **FATCA/CRS Reporting** | Updated reporting to new jurisdiction |

**Tax Reporting:** Annual self-assessment; foreign income must be declared

### Kenya

| Tax Type | Rate | Notes |

## 6.4 Tax Treaty Benefits

**UJAMAA DEFI PLATFORM investors may claim reduced withholding tax rates under applicable tax treaties.**

**To Claim Treaty Benefits:**
1. Submit tax residency certificate from home jurisdiction
2. Complete applicable tax forms (e.g., W-8BEN for US treaty benefits)
3. Update tax profile on UJAMAA platform

**Example Treaty Rates:**

| From | To | Standard Rate | Treaty Rate |
|------|----|---------------|-------------|
| Nigeria | UK | 10% | 7.5% |
| Kenya | UK | 10% | 5% |
| South Africa | Netherlands | 15% | 5% |
| Mauritius | India | 15% | 7.5% |
