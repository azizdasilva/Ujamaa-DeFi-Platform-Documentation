Impact Calculation

Jobs Created:

Direct Jobs = New hires attributable to financing Indirect Jobs = Direct Jobs × Multiplier (1.5x for manufacturing) Total Jobs = Direct + Indirect

GDP Contribution:

GDP Impact = Wages + Profits + Taxes + Local Supplier Spend

Women Empowerment:

Women-Led = Female CEO or >50% female ownership Women Employed = Female employees / Total employees

📄CASE STUDY: IMPACT STORY

Marie Kouassi — Textile Worker, Côte d'Ivoire

Background:
Marie, 34, is a single mother of three working at the GDIZ (Benin) textile factory financed by Ujamaa's ZARA pilot.

Before Financing:
- Seasonal employment (6 months/year)
- Income: €150/month (average)
- No skills training
- Children occasionally worked to supplement income

After Financing:
- Permanent employment (12 months/year)
- Income: €280/month (87% increase)
- Received quality control training
- Children in school full-time
- Saved €500 for home improvement

Quote:

ℹ️

"Before the factory got the financing, I never knew if I'd have work next month. Now I have a permanent job, I'm learning new skills, and my children are in school. The factory invested in new machines, and we're producing for international brands. I feel proud of my work."

Impact Summary:
- Income Increase: +87%
- Employment Stability: Seasonal → Permanent
- Skills Development: Quality control certification
- Child Welfare: Children in school, not working
- Financial Inclusion: Bank account, savings

📄ESG INTEGRATION

Exclusion Criteria

Ujamaa does NOT finance:

❌ Fossil fuel extraction or production
❌ Weapons manufacturing or distribution
❌ Tobacco production or distribution
❌ Gambling operations
❌ Adult entertainment
❌ Companies with child labor violations
❌ Companies with forced labor violations
❌ Companies on sanctions lists

Minimum Standards

All financed SMEs must comply with:

✅ Labor Standards: ILO core conventions (no child labor, no forced labor, freedom of association)
✅ Environmental Standards: Local environmental laws, no significant pollution
✅ Anti-CorruLPion: No bribery, transparent accounting
✅ Human Rights: Respect for human rights, no discrimination

Best Practices (Encouraged)

🌟 Living wages (above minimum)
🌟 Gender diversity in leadership
🌟 Environmental management systems
🌟 Community engagement programs
🌟 Employee training and development
