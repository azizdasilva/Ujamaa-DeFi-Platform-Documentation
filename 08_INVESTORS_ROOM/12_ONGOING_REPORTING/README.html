📄📖 Overview

This folder contains ongoing reporting templates for investor communications, including quarterly reports, annual reports, distribution notices, and tax statements.

📄📄 Documents

# Document Purpose Frequency
01 Quarterly Report Template Quarterly performance Quarterly
02 Annual Report Template Annual comprehensive Annual
03 Distribution Notice Template Distribution notices Per distribution
04 Tax Statement Template Tax documentation Annual

📄📊 Reporting Schedule

Quarterly Reports

Due: Within 30 days of quarter end

Contents:
- Portfolio performance summary
- NAV per share
- Yield distributions
- Market commentary
- Outlook

Template: Quarterly Report Template

Annual Reports

Due: Within 90 days of year end

Contents:
- Audited financial statements
- Full portfolio review
- Manager's discussion & analysis
- Risk management report
- Independent auditor's report

Template: Annual Report Template

Distribution Notices

Timing: 5 business days before distribution

Contents:
- Distribution amount per share
- Record date
- Payment date
- Reinvestment options (if applicable)

Template: Distribution Notice Template

Tax Statements

Due: By January 31 for prior year

Contents:
- Total distributions received
- Character of income (ordinary, capital gains)
- Foreign tax credits (if applicable)
- Tax lot information

Template: Tax Statement Template

📄📈 Performance Metrics

Key Performance Indicators

Metric Description Calculation
NAV per Share Net asset value Total Assets - Liabilities / Shares Outstanding
Total Return Investment return (Ending NAV - Beginning NAV + Distributions) / Beginning NAV
Annualized Return Annual return rate (1 + Total Return)^(365/Days) - 1
Yield Distribution yield Annual Distributions / Current NAV
Sharpe Ratio Risk-adjusted return (Return - Risk-Free Rate) / Standard Deviation

Risk Metrics

Metric Description Target
Default Rate % of defaulted assets < 2%
Recovery Rate % recovered from defaults > 60%
Concentration Largest single exposure < 10%
Liquidity Ratio Liquid assets / Total assets > 5%

📄📝 Reporting Standards

Quality Requirements

  • ✅ Accurate, verified data
  • ✅ Clear, concise language
  • ✅ Consistent formatting
  • ✅ Timely delivery
  • ✅ Regulatory compliance

Review Process

  1. Data Collection (T+5 days)
    - Portfolio data
    - Performance metrics
    - Transaction records

  2. Draft Preparation (T+10 days)
    - Initial draft
    - Charts and graphs
    - Commentary

  3. Internal Review (T+15 days)
    - Investment team review
    - Compliance review
    - Legal review (if required)

  4. Final Production (T+20 days)
    - Final edits
    - Formatting
    - Distribution preparation

  5. Distribution (T+30 days)
    - Email to investors
    - Portal upload
    - Regulatory filing (if required)

Disclosure Requirements

Material Events:
- Portfolio manager changes
- Significant losses (>10%)
- Regulatory actions
- Litigation
- Auditor changes

Timing: Within 5 business days
