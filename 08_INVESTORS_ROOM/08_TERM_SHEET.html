📄ECONOMIC TERMS

Management Fee

Parameter Detail
Rate 2.0% per annum
Calculation Daily accrual based on NAV
Collection Quarterly deduction from pool value
Payment Currency EUROD (deducted from NAV)

Performance Fee (Carried Interest)

Parameter Detail
Rate 20%
Hurdle Rate 8% preferred return (annualized)
Catch-up 100% catch-up to GP after hurdle
High-Water Mark Yes (no fees on recovered losses)
Calculation Per pool, per quarter
Clawback Yes (at fund level, upon liquidation)

Distribution Waterfall

1. Return of Capital → Investors (100%) 2. Preferred Return (8%) → Investors (100%) 3. Catch-up → GP (100%) until 20% of total profits 4. Remaining Profits → 80% Investors / 20% GP

Example Distribution

Assumptions: €100K investment, 11% annual yield, 1-year hold

Step Amount Recipient
Gross Yield €11,000 -
Management Fee (2%) -€2,000 Platform
Net Yield (before performance fee) €9,000 -
Preferred Return (8%) €8,000 Investor
Excess over Hurdle €1,000 -
Performance Fee (20% of €1,000) -€200 Platform
Investor Net Return €8,800 Investor
Total Platform Revenue €2,200 Platform
Investor Net Yield 8.8% After all fees
