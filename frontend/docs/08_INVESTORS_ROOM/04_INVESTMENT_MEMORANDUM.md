## 3. PROBLEM & SOLUTION

### 3.1 The Problem: African SME Financing Gap

**$331 Billion** — The African SME financing gap (World Bank, 2025)

#### 3.1.1 Traditional Bank Financing Barriers

| Barrier | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| **100%+ Collateral Required** | 80% of SMEs lack sufficient collateral |
| **18-24% Interest Rates** | Prohibitive for productive businesses |
| **6-12 Month Approval** | Too slow for working capital needs |
| **Complex Documentation** | SMEs lack financial reporting capacity |

#### 3.1.2 Private Equity Limitations

| Limitation | Impact |
|------------|--------|
| **$10M+ Minimum Tickets** | Too large for SME needs ($500K-€5M) |
| **20-30% Equity Dilution** | Founders reluctant to lose control |
| **5-7 Year Lock-up** | Inflexible for business cycles |
| **Complex Legal Structures** | High transaction costs |

#### 3.1.3 Unregulated DeFi Risks

| Risk | Impact |
|------|--------|
| **No Institutional Participation** | Pension funds, insurance cannot invest |
| **Anonymous Counterparties** | No KYC/AML, sanctions risk |
| **No Regulatory Oversight** | No investor protection |
| **High Smart Contract Risk** | $2B+ lost to exploits (2022-2025) |

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Risk** | Industrial borrower fails to repay financing |
| **Probability** | Medium (5-10% expected default rate) |
| **Impact** | Loss of principal + interest for pool |
| **Mitigation** | • GDIZ guarantee (primary recourse)<br>• 5% credit provision<br>• Diversification (40+ financings per pool)<br>• Collateral (Guarantee Token)<br>• OHADA legal enforcement |

#### 10.2.2 Smart Contract Risk

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Risk** | Bug or exploit in smart contract code |
| **Probability** | Low (audited, battle-tested ERC-3643) |
| **Impact** | Loss of funds, platform disruLPion |
| **Mitigation** | • CertiK/OpenZeppelin audits<br>• Formal verification<br>• Bug bounty ($100K-1M)<br>• Multisig governance<br>• Timelock upgrades (7-day delay)<br>• Emergency pause mechanism |

#### 10.2.3 FX Risk

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Risk** | EUR/African currency fluctuations |
| **Probability** | Medium (10-20% annual volatility) |
| **Impact** | Reduced yield for EUR investors |
| **Mitigation** | • EUROD denomination<br>• Natural hedge (export-oriented industrials)<br>• Optional FX hedging instruments<br>• Geographic diversification |

#### 10.2.4 Regulatory Risk

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Risk** | Regulatory changes affecting operations |
| **Probability** | Low (Mauritius FSC licensed, OHADA harmonized) |
| **Impact** | License revocation, operational restrictions |
| **Mitigation** | • Mauritius FSC CIS Manager License<br>• ERC-3643 compliance (MiCA, FATF)<br>• OHADA legal framework<br>• Proactive regulatory engagement |

#### 10.2.5 Political Risk

| Aspect | Detail |

| **Probability** | Medium (varies by country) |
