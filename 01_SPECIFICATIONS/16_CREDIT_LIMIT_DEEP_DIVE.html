2. Risk-Weighted Collateral Factors (Asset-Class Specific)

Instead of a flat 50%, the upgraded system uses Basel III-style haircuts per asset class, benchmarked against Aave V3, Morpho Blue, and traditional banking standards:

Asset ClassMinDefaultMaxRationale
RECEIVABLES70%75%80%Invoices — high liquidity, verifiable
INVENTORY50%55%60%Warehoused goods — moderate liquidity
EQUIPMENT40%45%50%Machinery — depreciation, illiquid
REAL_ESTATE60%65%70%Property — stable, illiquid
CONTRACT30%35%40%Offtake agreements — counterparty risk
WORKING_CAPITAL20%25%30%Intangible — no hard collateral
PROJECT_FINANCE20%25%30%Project-based — high execution risk
OTHER20%30%40%Unclassified — conservative default

All factors are admin-manageable via the Credit Limit Settings page.

2b. 5Cs Risk Scoring (0-100)

Based on the traditional banking 5 Cs of Credit framework, adapted for RWA tokenization:

DimensionWeightWhat It Measures
Character20%KYB/KYC quality, document completeness, verification depth
Capacity25%Cash flow, repayment history, loan completion rate
Capital15%Operator's equity, skin in the game, total invested
Collateral25%Asset quality, diversification, liquidity score
Conditions15%Sector outlook, jurisdiction risk, commodity volatility

Formula: Score = Character×0.20 + Capacity×0.25 + Capital×0.15 + Collateral×0.25 + Conditions×0.15

7. Upgraded Calculation Pipeline

Step 1: Verification Gate → KYB must be APPROVED
Step 2: Risk-Weighted Collateral → Σ(Asset_value × Asset_class_factor)
Step 3: 5Cs Risk Score → Weighted composite (0-100)
Step 4: Tier Multiplier → Based on score (AAA=1.0, A=0.8, etc.)
Step 5: Concentration → ±adjustment for diversification
Step 6: Reputation → Compounding multiplier from repayment history
Step 7: Global Cap → min(result, €500,000)

Final = Collateral_weighted × Tier_Multiplier × Concentration × Reputation
        (capped at global max)

8. Example Calculations

Example 1: Green Cotton SA — Real Operator (Upgraded)

📋 Profile
  • Role: INDUSTRIAL_OPERATOR | Operator Type: AGRICULTURAL
  • KYB Status: APPROVED | Certified Assets: €1,000,000 (Inventory + Receivables)
  • Repayment: 1 loan fully repaid on time (€250K → €270K)
Step 2: Risk-Weighted Collateral
  Inventory (€400K × 55%) = €220K
  Receivables (€600K × 75%) = €450K
  → RWC = €670K (vs €500K at flat 50%)

Step 3: 5Cs Risk Score
  Character: 65 | Capacity: 100 | Capital: 30 | Collateral: 70 | Conditions: 75
  → Composite = 65×0.20 + 100×0.25 + 30×0.15 + 70×0.25 + 75×0.15 = 71.25 → Rating: A

Step 4: Tier Multiplier → 0.8× (A rating)
  Base = €670K × 0.8 = €536K

Step 5: Concentration → No penalty (diversified across 2 asset classes)

Step 6: Reputation → 1 on-time repayment → +2% → 1.02×
  €536K × 1.02 = €546.7K

Step 7: Global Cap → min(€546.7K, €500K) = €500K

→ Final Credit Limit: €500,000
→ Available: €500,000 (loan fully repaid)

11. Admin Management

All parameters are managed via the /admin/settings/credit-limits page in the frontend, which provides:

  • ⚙️ Global settings and tier thresholds
  • ⚖️ 5Cs weights (must sum to 1.0)
  • 📊 Collateral factors per asset class (with min/max/default ranges)
  • ⭐ Reputation system parameters (bonus/penalty percentages)
  • 🎯 Concentration risk thresholds
  • 🔄 Mass recalculation button for all operators

Each parameter change creates a new version for audit trail. Previous versions are preserved.
