1. What Is a Credit Limit?

A credit limit is the maximum amount (in EUR/EUROD) an industrial operator can borrow on the Ujamaa DeFi Platform at any given time. It represents the operator's borrowing capacity and is automatically calculated based on their business profile, certified assets, and repayment history.

Think of it like a credit card limit: you can draw down any amount up to your limit, and as you repay loans, that capacity becomes available again.

2. How Is It Calculated?

The credit limit is determined by three factors:

2.1 KYB Tier Base Limit

Your company type determines a base credit tier:

Business TypeTierBase Limit
Farming / AgricultureTIER_1€10,000
Trading / WholesaleTIER_1€10,000
Manufacturing / ProductionTIER_2€50,000
Large InstitutionalTIER_3€200,000

2.2 Collateral Credit (Asset-Class-Specific)

When you submit and certify assets, the system applies risk-weighted collateral factors based on asset class (benchmarked against Aave V3, Morpho Blue, and Basel III):

Asset ClassFactorExample (€100K)
Receivables (Invoices)75%€75K credit
Inventory (Warehoused)55%€55K credit
Equipment45%€45K credit
Real Estate65%€65K credit
Contracts/Other30-35%€30-35K credit

All factors are admin-manageable via Credit Limit Settings.

2.3 5Cs Risk Scoring

Each operator receives a composite risk score (0-100) based on:

  • Character (20%) — KYB quality, document completeness
  • Capacity (25%) — Cash flow, repayment history
  • Capital (15%) — Operator equity, skin in the game
  • Collateral (25%) — Asset quality, diversification
  • Conditions (15%) — Sector outlook, jurisdiction risk

2.4 Tier Rating & Multiplier

Based on the composite score: AAA (90-100) = 1.0×, AA = 0.9×, A = 0.8×, BBB = 0.7×, BB = 0.5×, B = 0.3×, C (<40) = denied.

2.5 Reputation & Concentration

On-time repayments add +2% compounding (max +20%). Defaults subtract -50%. Concentrated collateral gets penalties; diversified gets bonuses.

2.6 The Formula

Step 1: Risk-Weighted Collateral → Σ(Asset_value × Asset_class_factor)
Step 2: 5Cs Risk Score → Weighted composite (0-100)
Step 3: Tier Multiplier → Based on score (AAA=1.0, A=0.8, etc.)
Step 4: Concentration → ±adjustment
Step 5: Reputation → Compounding multiplier
Step 6: Global Cap → min(result, €500,000)

Final = RWC × Tier_Multiplier × Concentration × Reputation

3. The KYC/KYB Gate

⚠️ Important

Credit limits are only active when both KYC (Know Your Customer) and KYB (Know Your Business) verifications are APPROVED.

KYC StatusKYB StatusCredit LimitCan Request Financing?
APPROVEDAPPROVED✅ Calculated✅ Yes
APPROVEDPENDING€0❌ No
PENDINGPENDING€0❌ No
AnyREJECTED€0❌ No

While pending, you can still:

  • View your dashboard
  • Preview asset submission
  • View company information

But you cannot request financing, deploy funds, or have a credit limit assigned.

4. How to View Your Credit Limit

4.1 On Your Dashboard

When logged in as an Industrial Operator, your dashboard displays:

┌──────────────┬──────────────┬──────────────────┬─────────────┐
│ Credit Limit │ Outstanding  │ Active Financings│ Fundraising │
│   €185,000   │   €20,000    │        1         │      0      │
└──────────────┴──────────────┴──────────────────┴─────────────┘
  • Credit Limit: Your maximum borrowing capacity
  • Outstanding: Amount currently borrowed (principal - repayments)
  • Available Credit: Credit Limit - Outstanding (shown in the sidebar)

4.2 Via API

# Get your credit limit
curl -H "Authorization: Bearer your-email@example.com" \
  http://localhost:8000/api/v2/credit-limits/me

# Response includes credit_limit, available_credit, and a breakdown

5. For Admins: How to Override Limits

Administrators can manually set or reset any user's credit limit.

5.1 Override a User's Limit

curl -X PUT \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer admin-token-mvp" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{"credit_limit": 150000, "reason": "Strategic partner approval"}' \
  http://localhost:8000/api/v2/credit-limits/users/5

5.2 Reset to Auto-Calculated

curl -X POST \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer admin-token-mvp" \
  http://localhost:8000/api/v2/credit-limits/users/5/reset

5.3 View Any User's Credit Details

curl -H "Authorization: Bearer admin-token-mvp" \
  http://localhost:8000/api/v2/credit-limits/users/5

5.4 Audit Trail

Every credit limit change is recorded in the user_credit_limits table with:

  • Who changed it (reason code)
  • When it was changed
  • The detailed breakdown of the calculation

6.2 Data Flow

  1. User logs in → Frontend stores session in sessionStorage
  2. Dashboard loads → Calls GET /db/investors/me
  3. Backend calculates → credit_limit_service.calculate_credit_limit()
  4. Response includes → credit_limit, available_credit, verification_gate
  5. Frontend displays → Stats cards with status-aware banners
  6. Financing request → Backend checks available_credit >= requested_amount
  7. If exceeds → Returns 400 error with message

Every credit limit change creates an immutable record in user_credit_limits:

  • Cannot be deleted (CASCADE on user delete only)
  • Includes reason code and details
  • Timestamped for compliance

7.3 Admin Privileges

  • Only users with role = 'ADMIN' can access admin endpoints
  • Returns 403 Forbidden for non-admin requests
  • Admin overrides are logged with the admin's email in the details

8. FAQ

My credit limit seems low — what can I do?

  1. Certify more assets: Higher-quality assets (receivables at 75%) count more than lower-quality ones (equipment at 45%)
  2. Repay on time: Each on-time repayment adds +2% to your limit (compounding, max +20%)
  3. Diversify your collateral: Multiple asset classes with no single class >30% earns a +5% bonus
  4. Improve your 5Cs score: Complete KYB documentation, maintain good cash flow, demonstrate skin in the game
  5. Contact admin: For strategic partnerships, an admin can override the limit up to €500K

Can I increase my limit by depositing more collateral?

Yes. The amount added depends on the asset class — receivables add 75%, inventory 55%, equipment 45%, real estate 65%. To certify an asset: go to "Submit Asset for Tokenization", fill in asset details, wait for compliance review and certification. Once certified, your credit limit recalculates automatically.

Does loan repayment increase my limit immediately?

Yes. Each on-time repayment adds +2% to your limit (compounding, max +20%). Your outstanding decreases and available_credit increases simultaneously.

What happens if my KYB gets rejected?

Your credit limit immediately becomes €0. Any pending financing requests will be blocked. You'll see a "Rejected" banner on your dashboard with contact information for the compliance team.

Can I see the breakdown of how my limit was calculated?

Yes. The API response includes a breakdown object with all components (tier, base limit, collateral credit, repayment bonus, etc.).

Does my credit limit expire?

No. Credit limits do not have an expiration date by default. However, they are recalculated when new assets are certified, repayments are made, KYB status changes, or an admin manually overrides.
