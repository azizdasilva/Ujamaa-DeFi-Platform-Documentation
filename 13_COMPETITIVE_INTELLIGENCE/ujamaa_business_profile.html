💎1. Core Value Proposition

Problem Solved

Africa faces a $331 billion SME financing gap (World Bank, 2025). Traditional financing channels fail African SMEs due to:

  • 100%+ collateral requirements (excludes 80% of SMEs)
  • 18-24% interest rates (prohibitive for productive borrowing)
  • 6-12 month approval processes (too slow for working capital needs)
  • $10M+ private equity minimums (exceed SME needs of $500K-€5M)

Ujamaa DeFi Platform's Solution

First ERC-3643 compliant liquidity pool platform for African industrial financing, enabling institutional investors to earn 10-15% APR (EUROD-denominated) by providing diversified financing to productive African businesses.

Core Innovation

  • uLP Token: Yield-bearing ERC-3643 token representing ownership in diversified financing pools
  • Guarantee Token: Non-transferable ERC-3643 NFT representing certified merchandise/collateral
  • Liquidity Pools: Diversified pools (Industrie, Agriculture, Trade Finance, Renewable Energy) with automated yield distribution
  • Institutional Infrastructure: Fireblocks custody, BIIC/MCB bank escrow, Mauritius FSC regulation
