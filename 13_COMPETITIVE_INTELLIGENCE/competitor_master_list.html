🔍Key Findings

Market Concentration

  • RWA Tokenization is the most competitive category (10 players identified)
  • African DeFi is emerging but fragmented (6 players, none with full Ujamaa feature set)
  • Institutional Lending dominated by Maple ($4B+) and Goldfinch

TVL Distribution

  • Top 3 by TVL: Aave ($57B+), Maple ($4B+), BlackRock BUIDL ($517M)
  • Ujamaa DeFi Platform Target: €50M Year 1 (significantly smaller than established players)
  • Niche Opportunity: African SME financing is underserved relative to US/EU RWA

Funding Landscape

  • Most Funded: Securitize ($100M+), Chipper Cash ($300M+), LemFi ($85M)
  • VC Interest: a16z (Goldfinch), Highland Europe (LemFi), Coinbase Ventures (multiple)
  • African Fintech Rebound: $3.2B raised in 2025 (up from 2022-2024 slump)

Technology Trends

  • ERC-3643 Adoption: Tokeny, Polymath using similar compliance standards
  • Multi-Chain Deployment: Centrifuge operates on 8 chains; Ujamaa uses Polygon+Ethereum
  • Institutional Custody: Fireblocks integration common among Class 1/2 players
